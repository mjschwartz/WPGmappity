Description:
Abstract: This map layer shows Amtrak intercity railroad passenger terminals in theUnited States and Canada. It includes train stations and thruway busstations. There are no Amtrak stations in Alaska or Hawaii. The data area subset of data available from the Research and Innovative TechnologyAdministration's Bureau of Transportation Statistics (RITA/BTS)<http://www.bts.gov/gis/>.
Purpose: These data are intended for geographic display and analysis at thenational level, and for large regional areas. The data should bedisplayed and analyzed at scales appropriate for 1:2,000,000-scale data.No responsibility is assumed by the National Atlas of the United States inthe use of these data.

Data Quality Information:
Attribute Value Accuracy Information:
Attribute Accuracy Report: Attribute data were extracted from the station listings in theSpring/Summer 2000 Amtrak National and Summer/Fall 2000 Amtrak NortheastTimetables, and reflect station characteristics as of July 9, 2000.Additional data, as required, were obtained from earlier year timetables.
Logical Consistency Report: No checks for topological consistency were performed on this map layer.
Completeness Report: The database includes all Amtrak railroad and thruway bus stations listed inthe Amtrak timetables mentioned above. Regional commuter railroad stationsthat are not also served by Amtrak are not included in this version; thosestations are currently included in the Fixed-Guideway Transit Networkdatabase. The database does not include information on passenger boardings;those data are considered to be proprietary by Amtrak.
